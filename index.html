About Team United FC

Founded in 1998 by Clive Steve, Team United FC was established with a vision to bring together talented young athletes and build a strong, united soccer community. Over the years, the club has grown into more than just a team—it is a family rooted in discipline, development, and opportunity. Team United FC is dedicated to nurturing talent, promoting teamwork, and empowering players both on and off the field. With a commitment to excellence and community impact, the club continues to inspire the next generation of footballers to reach their full potential.
