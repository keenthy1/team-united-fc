About Team United FC

Founded in 1998 by Clive Steve, Team United FC was created to bring together talented young athletes and build a strong, unified soccer community. The club is deeply rooted in values of discipline, teamwork, and personal growth. Over the years, it has developed into a platform where players are empowered to reach their full potential both on and off the field. With a strong focus on mentorship, development, and competitive excellence, Team United FC continues to shape the next generation of footballers while fostering a sense of family and purpose within the community.
